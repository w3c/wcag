Consistent Help

A

New

For each web page within a set of web pages that provides one or more of following ways of finding help, access to at least one form of help is included in the same relative order on each page:

  • Human contact details;
  • Human contact mechanism;
  • Self-help option;
  • A fully automated contact mechanism.

Access to help mechanisms may be provided directly on the page, or may be provided via a direct link to a different page containing the information.
