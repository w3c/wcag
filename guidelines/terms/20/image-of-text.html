image of text

text that has been rendered in a non-text form (e.g., an image) in order to achieve a particular visual effect

This does not include text that is incidental – where the text is not part of the essential content being conveyed by the picture. For instance, in a photograph of a person, the person happens to be wearing a name tag; the name tag itself is incidental, as the purpose of the photograph is not to identify the person, and the text on the name tag is not treated as an image of text subject to evaluation against success criteria.
