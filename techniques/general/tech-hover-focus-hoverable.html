Making content on focus or hover hoverable

Metadata

When to Use

The Technique is applicable to any technology that supports the display of additional content on pointer hover.

Description

Content that is displayed in a popup when users move the pointer over a trigger or focus the trigger with the keboard needs to stay visible when users move their pointer over the popup content. Low vision users using screen magnification often see only a small part of the screen. This means that the popup content may not be fully visible in the currently visible section. Often, the position of content visible in the enlarged section changes based on users' mouse movement, so magnification users may move their mouse over partly visible popup content to read it. Web content should therefore ensue that popup content stays vsisible when the pointer moves away from the trigger to (mostly adjacent) popup content.
